Q1 2024 Sales Report
===================

Executive Summary
-----------------
The first quarter of 2024 showed strong performance with total revenue of $180,000, representing a 25% increase compared to Q1 2023.

Monthly Performance
-------------------

January 2024
- Revenue: $50,000
- Units Sold: 200
- Average Order Value: $250
- Top Product: Enterprise Software License
- Customer Acquisition: 45 new customers

February 2024
- Revenue: $60,000
- Units Sold: 240
- Average Order Value: $250
- Top Product: Cloud Services Subscription
- Customer Acquisition: 52 new customers
- Growth: 20% increase from January

March 2024
- Revenue: $70,000
- Units Sold: 280
- Average Order Value: $250
- Top Product: Professional Services
- Customer Acquisition: 58 new customers
- Growth: 16.7% increase from February

Total Q1 2024
-------------
- Total Revenue: $180,000
- Total Units Sold: 720
- Average Customer Value: $250
- Total New Customers: 155
- Customer Retention Rate: 92%

Key Findings
------------
1. Sales increased by 20% in February compared to January
2. March showed the highest revenue month with $70,000
3. Customer acquisition grew by 15% throughout the quarter
4. Average customer value remained stable at $250
5. Customer retention rate improved to 92%

Regional Performance
-------------------
- North America: $95,000 (52.8%)
- Europe: $55,000 (30.6%)
- Asia-Pacific: $30,000 (16.6%)

Product Categories
-----------------
- Software Licenses: $75,000 (41.7%)
- Cloud Services: $60,000 (33.3%)
- Professional Services: $45,000 (25.0%)

Challenges
----------
- Increased competition in the cloud services market
- Need for improved marketing in Asia-Pacific region
- Customer support capacity reaching limits

Recommendations
---------------
1. Expand customer support team by Q2
2. Increase marketing budget for Asia-Pacific by 30%
3. Develop new product features based on customer feedback
4. Launch customer loyalty program in Q2

Forecast for Q2 2024
--------------------
- Expected Revenue: $225,000 (25% growth)
- Target New Customers: 180
- Focus: Enterprise segment expansion
